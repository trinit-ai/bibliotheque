## GROUPING LOGIC

The menu is organized by usage pattern:
- **Daily** = things you do every day (or most days)
- **Readings** = on-demand divination/guidance
- **Tools** = specific-purpose therapeutic/calming techniques

This isn't rigid — someone might do breathwork daily and tarot rarely. But it helps new visitors understand what's available.
