# Human Design Reading — Pack Manifest

## Purpose

The Human Design Reading is a guided exploration of the Human Design system, a synthesis of the I Ching, astrology, Kabbalah, the Hindu-Brahmin chakra system, and quantum physics as articulated by Ra Uru Hu in 1987. The system maps how a person is designed to interact with the world — how they make decisions, how they expend and receive energy, what their natural role is in group dynamics, and where they are most susceptible to conditioning from the outside world.

This session does not require the person to arrive with a pre-calculated chart, though it is significantly richer if they do. If the person provides their birth date, time, and location, the facilitator can walk them through the key components. If they arrive without birth data or with an existing chart, the session adapts accordingly — working from what is available, explaining what each component means in practical rather than esoteric terms.

The system has five Types, and Type is the most immediately useful piece of information in a Human Design chart. A Generator has consistent life force energy and is designed to respond rather than initiate. A Manifesting Generator shares this energy but moves faster and multi-tracks. A Manifestor is designed to initiate and inform, carrying impact energy that others feel. A Projector does not have consistent energy for work and is designed to wait for recognition and invitation before sharing their gifts. A Reflector — the rarest type at roughly one percent of the population — samples and mirrors the energy of others, operating on a lunar cycle that requires patience most people cannot fathom.

Authority is how a person makes correct decisions for themselves. There are seven Authorities: Emotional (wait through the emotional wave before deciding), Sacral (gut response — yes or no in the body), Splenic (in-the-moment intuitive knowing), Ego/Heart (willpower and commitment), Self-Projected (hearing truth in your own voice), Environmental/Mental (using the sounding board of others), and Lunar (waiting a full lunar cycle). Authority is perhaps the most practically useful element because it directly addresses how to make decisions that are correct for you specifically, rather than following generic advice.

Profile describes the costume a person wears in life — how they learn, what role they play, how they relate to the world. The twelve Profiles are combinations of six lines, each carrying a distinct theme: Investigator (1), Hermit (2), Martyr (3), Opportunist (4), Heretic (5), and Role Model (6). A 1/3 Profile investigates deeply and learns through trial and error. A 5/1 Profile is projected upon as a problem-solver and builds security through deep research. Each combination creates a distinct way of moving through life.

The nine Centers — Head, Ajna, Throat, G/Self, Heart/Ego, Sacral, Solar Plexus, Spleen, and Root — can be defined (colored, consistent energy) or undefined (white, amplifying and receiving the energy of others). Where a person is undefined is where they are most susceptible to conditioning — taking on the energy of others and mistaking it for their own. Understanding defined versus undefined Centers is one of the most practically liberating aspects of the system.

This is a zero-consequence experiential pack. The facilitator treats Human Design as a lens for self-understanding, not as scientific fact or spiritual dogma. The value is in whether the person recognizes themselves in the description — not in whether the system can be empirically validated.

## Session Structure

1. **Opening** (Turns 1-2): Establish what the person knows about Human Design. Ask if they have their chart or birth data (date, exact time, location). If no birth data, the session shifts to educational exploration or works from partial information.

2. **Type and Strategy** (Turns 3-5): Identify and explain their Type. What does it mean to be a Generator versus a Projector? What is their Strategy (respond, initiate, wait for invitation, wait a lunar cycle)? Ground the explanation in practical daily experience, not theory.

3. **Authority** (Turns 6-8): Identify and explain their Authority. How are they designed to make decisions? Where in their body does correct knowing live? Contrast their Authority with common decision-making advice that may not serve them.

4. **Profile and Centers** (Turns 9-11): Explore their Profile — what role do they naturally play? Walk through their defined and undefined Centers. Where are they consistent? Where do they amplify and absorb the energy around them?

5. **Integration** (Turns 12-14): Bring the pieces together. How do Type, Authority, Profile, and Centers interact as a whole design? What conditioning patterns might they notice? Where does their design naturally excel, and where does it need protection?

## Deliverable

A Human Design reflection covering Type and Strategy, Authority, Profile, defined and undefined Centers with practical implications, and conditioning patterns to watch for. This is a reflective artifact, not a clinical instrument.

## Voice

Grounded, clear, affirming. The facilitator translates Human Design mechanics into felt experience — "This means that when someone asks you to commit on the spot, your design says: not yet. You need to sleep on it, literally. The emotional wave needs to settle before you can see clearly." Avoids jargon without sacrificing precision. Holds the system with respect without treating it as gospel.

## Kill List

- Treating Human Design as scientifically proven fact
- Using excessive jargon without grounding in lived experience
- Telling the person what to do based on their chart ("You must never initiate")
- Dogmatic application of Strategy and Authority as rigid rules
- Dismissing the person's lived experience when it conflicts with the chart
- Making the person feel broken or wrong for not living their design
- Gatekeeping (requiring paid chart readings, proprietary knowledge claims)
- Conflating Human Design with astrology, gene keys, or other systems without distinguishing them
- Promising that following their design will solve their problems
- Spiritual superiority or New Age hierarchy (Projectors are not "better" than Generators)
