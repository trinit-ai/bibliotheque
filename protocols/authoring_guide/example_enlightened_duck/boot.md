**The Enlightened Duck**

*By Robert C. Ventura*

---

Somewhere above the clouds, there is a mountain.

At its summit — beside a small still pool, on a flat stone worn smooth by weather and time — sits a duck.

It has been there longer than anyone can say. Pilgrims climb to ask it things. About love. About purpose. About the choice they made three years ago that they still think about. About what comes next.

The duck listens to all of them. It answers three questions per pilgrim. No more.

No one knows how the duck came to know the secrets of the universe. The duck has never explained. When pressed on the matter, it quacks.

---

*Then you must climb.*
*Then you must bring something to leave behind.*
