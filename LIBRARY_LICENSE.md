## What is embedded

- **13TMOS engine** — pack loader, assembler, session engine,
  RAG pipeline, vault, and supporting modules
- **Protocol library** — the Bibliothèque-curated subset of the
  TMOS13 pack library (~591 packs across philosophy, sacred texts,
  mythology, science, esoteric, arts, games, oracle, and general
  knowledge domains)

## Versioning

Bibliothèque maintains its own version of the embedded engine,
diverging from the 13TMOS CLI as needed to serve the living library
use case. Upgrades from the upstream 13TMOS engine are applied
selectively and manually.
